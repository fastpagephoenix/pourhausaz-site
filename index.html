About Pour Haus

Check Date Availability

Pour Haus is a family-run tap trailer and charcuterie bar based in Scottsdale, Arizona. We blend clean, modern design with warm, organic details – think wood, linen, and candlelight – to create a laid-back, elevated bar experience at your home, venue, or office.

Our trailer can pour your favorite beers, ciders, or batched cocktails while our boards bring together cured meats, cheeses, fresh fruit, and small bites that look as good as they taste.

How It Works

Plan My Event

Pour Haus is a mobile setup – we bring the tap trailer, bars, decor, and service to your location. You choose the vibe, we handle the flow.

  1. Share the basics. Date, time, location, guest count, and what you’re celebrating.
  2. Choose your service style. Tap trailer only, charcuterie only, or the full Pour Haus experience.
  3. We build a simple plan. We’ll outline set up, menu, timeline, and staffing so you know exactly what to expect.
  4. Event day. We arrive early, style the space, serve your guests, and break everything down when the party’s over.

We’ll work within your venue’s rules and local requirements. You provide the beverages, we provide the trailer, styling, and service.

FAQ

Ask A Question

What is a tap trailer?

A tap trailer is a mobile bar on wheels with built-in taps. We bring the trailer, hook up your kegs or batched cocktails, and serve directly from the taps at your event.

Do you provide the alcohol?

You supply the alcohol and we supply the trailer, decor, ice, bar tools, and service. This keeps everything simple and flexible for venues and hosts.

Where do you serve?

We’re based in Scottsdale and serve the greater Phoenix area. For events outside this area, send us the details and we’ll see what’s possible.

What kinds of events do you do?

Backyard parties, weddings, rehearsal dinners, showers, corporate events, pop-ups, and styled shoots. If people are gathering, we can likely bring the Pour Haus trailer.
