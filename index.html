FAQ

Ask A Question

Do you provide the alcohol?

No. Right now, you provide all alcoholic beverages. We provide the tap trailer, setup, styling, service, and break down.

Where do you operate?

We’re based in Scottsdale and serve the greater Phoenix area. Have a location further out? Reach out — travel may be available.

What types of events do you do?

Weddings, birthdays, showers, corporate events, backyard parties, styled shoots, and more. If people are gathering, we can likely pour for it.

How far in advance should we book?

Popular dates fill quickly. We recommend reaching out 3–6 months in advance, but don’t hesitate to ask about last-minute openings.
