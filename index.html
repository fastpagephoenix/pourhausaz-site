About Pour Haus

Pour Haus is a luxury mobile bar + charcuterie service that brings the speakeasy experience directly to your event. From weddings to backyard brunches, we make any occasion unforgettable.

How It Works

We bring the bar, the vibes, and the hospitality. You supply the alcohol — we handle the presentation, service, mixers, garnishes, and all the magic behind the bar.

No liquor license? No problem. You provide the bottles. We provide the experience.
