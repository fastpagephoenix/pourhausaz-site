About Pour Haus

Pour Haus is a luxury mobile tap + charcuterie experience bringing modern rustic hospitality to your event. Whether you’re hosting a wedding, backyard brunch, or evening party, we deliver the aesthetic, the tap trailer, and service — you simply supply the beverages.

How It Works

  1. Share the basics. Tell us the date, location, guest count, and vibe.
  2. Choose your service style. Tap trailer, charcuterie, or both.
  3. We build your plan. Setup, menu, timeline, staffing — all handled.
  4. Event day. We arrive early, style the space, serve, and tear down.

You provide the beverages. We provide the trailer, styling, and service.
