About Pour Haus

Pour Haus is a family-run mobile bar based in Scottsdale, Arizona. We bring the bar to you – complete with a styled setup, professional service, and a menu that works for early mornings, late nights, and everything in between.

In the mornings, we focus on breakfast-style service with mini pancakes and coffee options. In the evenings, we flip the lights and create a cozy bar atmosphere with beverage service and elevated bites that feel right at home at a speakeasy.

How It Works

Pour Haus is a mobile bar & food service that sets up at your location – homes, venues, offices, event spaces, and more. We bring the bar structure, the look, and the service team. You provide the space and we handle the experience.

  1. Tell us about your event. Guest count, date, time, and vibe.
  2. Choose your service style. Breakfast bar, evening bar, or a mix of both.
  3. We build a custom service plan. We outline timing, menu, and staffing so you know what to expect.
  4. Day-of setup. We arrive early, set up the bar area, and stay through service and breakdown.

We work with you and your venue’s rules to make sure all beverage service is handled properly. Our team focuses on bartending and food service – we can tailor the setup around what you plan to provide for your guests.

FAQ

What is a mobile bar?

A mobile bar is a bar setup that travels to your event. We bring the bar structure, supplies, and team to create a bar experience at your location instead of you having to go to a venue.

Do you provide the alcohol?

We currently focus on bar setup, staffing, and service. We’ll work with you on planning what’s needed for your event so everything is handled properly within your venue’s rules and local requirements.

What areas do you serve?

We primarily serve Scottsdale and the greater Phoenix area. For events outside this area, reach out and we’ll see what’s possible.

What types of events do you do?

Weddings, birthdays, corporate events, holiday parties, graduation celebrations, and private in-home events. If people are gathering, we can likely build a bar experience for it.

How far in advance should we book?

The earlier the better, especially for weekends and peak seasons. Reach out with your date and we’ll let you know our availability.
