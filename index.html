About Pour Haus

Check Date Availability

Pour Haus is a luxury mobile tap + charcuterie experience bringing warm, modern rustic hospitality to your event. Whether you’re hosting a wedding, backyard brunch, or evening party, we bring the aesthetic, the tap trailer, and the service — you simply provide the beverages.

We focus on natural textures, clean design, and elevated presentation so your guests feel welcomed, relaxed, and taken care of.

How It Works

Plan My Event
  1. Share the basics. Date, time, location, guest count, and vibe.
  2. Choose your service style. Tap trailer, charcuterie, or both.
  3. We build your plan. Setup, timeline, staffing — all handled.
  4. Event day. We arrive early, style the space, serve, and clean up.

You provide the beverages. We provide the trailer, styling, and service.
